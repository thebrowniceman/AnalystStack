CRM

CRM software (customer relationship management software), in some cases also called IRM (investor relationship management software), helps investors track and manage all interactions in a single system of records. The CRM will record all interactions (e.G. emails) between investors, founders, and third parties. It makes communication also way easier by showing all relevant data in one place.
